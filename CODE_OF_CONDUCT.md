# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Short version: be decent. We want the project to be a useful place for developers who ship streaming systems. Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory jokes are not welcome and won't be tolerated in issues, PRs, discussions, or any related space.

If you see behavior that crosses the line, email `sderosiaux@conduktor.io` directly. Reports are handled privately.
